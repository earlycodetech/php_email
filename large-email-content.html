About Early Code

Early Code was founded in 2019 to coach teens and young adults in coding, software and hardware development. In Early Code, coding is considered the preliminary skill to building technologies. Coaching and helping people to acquire the tech skills they need is one of our goals to get Nigeria and the entire African continent to become a tech developing region.

Early Code focuses more on practical skills to ensure that participants and trainees who enroll for any Early Code training can become proficient in their chosen paths.

Science, Technology, Engineering and Mathematics (STEM) are the majors goals of Early Code in mentoring and coaching trainees.

Early Code is a registered enterprise with the Corporate Affairs Commission (CAC) of Nigeria with RC: 2664079.
